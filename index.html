Leading with excellence

The College, founded in 2005, as the commerce college under MJP Rohilkhand University, Bareilly has emerged as a unique institution progressing towards unprecedented heights. The college has six regular courses to offer- B.Com., B.Com. Honours, B.Com. Computer, B.Com. Finance, B.B.A. and B.C.A. Today we have about 1400 students in a beautiful and a well maintained campus. Our growth is not limited to the number of students, we believe in a multifaceted growth. The number of students, faculty members, teacher-to-student ratio, courses being offered to the students, facilities on campus, cultural, social and all other activities, exposure to the faculty members and students, various academic events, and our contribution to the society are all growing. Our drive for excellence comes from our own inner self, and yet, it is collective.

Nurturing tomorrow's leaders today

Being a student at Regional College is about so much more than getting qualifications. You will have the opportunity to get involved in enjoyable and worthwhile activities, make new friends, develop yourself and have a great time. Regional College is committed to offering the skills and knowledge to ensure that you stand out from the crowd and are able to secure your chosen job. The College has been fortunate, right from its inception in having a highly qualified, efficient and dedicated teaching fraternity. They have proved themselves to be fit instruments in moulding the personalities and careers of their students, always motivating the students in the pursuit of excellence and inspiring them with lofty ideals of social and national awareness. Students hence have an opportunity to learn from some of the top scholars in their disciplines who are dedicated both to quality education and to expanding our understanding of business and economic issues. A careful blend of outreach and classroom instruction creates an advantage for our students that is hard to duplicate and always keep them ahead. The entire learning process at Regional College is focused on inculcating in students, sensitivity to real-world issues and an ability to become leaders in their chosen fields. We want our students to create wealth, help create wealth and distribute that wealth in the form of quality products and services so that they touch the lives of masses positively and meaningfully, without sacrificing anything of theirs. We want our students to be wealthy, healthy and happy.
